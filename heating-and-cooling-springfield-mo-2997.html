    Factors to Keep in Mind When Choosing HVAC Systems

    When evaluate heating and cooling systems, energy performance must become a critical factor. In addition, consider the size of your home; an appropriately sized system should ensure optimal performance and comfort. Oversized units can cause short cycling, while too small ones may have difficulty to reach desired temperatures.

Every type offers its own set of benefits and cons. For instance, ductless systems tend to be more efficient for homes without ductwork, while central systems tend to be better suited for larger spaces.
